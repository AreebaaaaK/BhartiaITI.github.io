About Bhartia ITI


General Information

Affiliated with DGT- National Council of Vocational Training, Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship, Government of India.

Address: Nai Abadi, Kidwai Nagar, Etah (U.P.) - 207001, India.


Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide high-quality vocational training and education to empower individuals with skills for employment and entrepreneurship.

Our vision is to be a leading institute in skill development, contributing significantly to India's socio-economic growth.


History

Bhartiya ITI was established by Mr.Mohmmad Aslam Khan and Mr.Rasool Ahmed Khan in 2005 with the aim of bridging the skill gap in various industries. Over the years, we have trained numerous individuals and contributed to their personal and professional growth.


Courses We offer

We offer three courses for ITI

  • Electrician
  • Fitter
  • Electronic Mechanic

Our Main Focus

  • Affiliated with NIELIT for Olevel,CCC courses
  • Largest Computer Lab for all online exams facility
  • We have tie up with TCS and NIELIT and Vensysco for online exams
  • We have MOU with Ashraj foundation for placements
  • Training partner forn NSDC for short term courses
  • Our Authorized partnership with BOSCH company which offers BRIDGE(Bosch’s Response to India’s Development and Growth through Employability Enhancement) with different different training beneficial for students.
  • We also have partnership with Vigyan Club
